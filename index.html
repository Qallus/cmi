Our Project Process

The Process

01

Consultation

We listen to your ideas, goals, and concerns — taking time to understand your vision and what you hope to achieve with your project.

02

Assessment

We take a sketch or plan, verify its feasibility, and complete a high-level budget to set clear expectations from the start.

03

Design & Planning

Our architects, designers, and project managers craft a detailed scope, realistic budget, and schedule — tailored to your unique vision.

04

Assemble

We put pen to paper — engaging subcontractors and procuring materials to set the build in motion.

05

Construction

Our team transforms your vision into reality with open communication at every step — then hands it over for you to Enjoy your new space.

What We Do

Construction Without
the Stress

Most construction headaches come from poor coordination, missed deadlines, surprise change orders, subcontractors out of sync. CMI's project management service puts an experienced professional in charge of every moving part, so you don't have to be.

We serve as your owner's representative and general contractor, handling scheduling, procurement, subcontractor management, inspections, and budget tracking. You stay informed through weekly updates and a real-time project portal, without being buried in the details.
